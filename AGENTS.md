## Centre of Gravity

`dev` is the centre of gravity for the Workinabox organisation. It is the primary tool developers interact with and the place where the state of the whole organisation is understood and surfaced. When making changes in any other repo, consider the impact on the whole: does `dev` need to be updated to reflect, support, or orchestrate that change? If so, those changes to `dev` should be part of the same body of work.
